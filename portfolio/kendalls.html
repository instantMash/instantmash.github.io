Kendall's pioneer distributing

In 2014, a family-owned beverage distribution company that has been in business since 1939 decided to branch out in beer and wine, and they launched Kendall's Pioneer Distributing. Kendall's is like a talent scout for craft beverages in the Pacific Northwest. I made their logo and branding reflect their combination of tradition and forward-thinking style.
