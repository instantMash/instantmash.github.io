L&M Policy Research Report

L&M Policy Research released a report synthesizing lessons learned from a decade of research on consumer and patient perceptions of health care quality. I worked with them to create the design and layout of this 32-page booklet. This is the kind of challenge that I enjoy most: taking complex technical information and using good design to make it accessible and inviting to the reader.
