Backwoods Brewing

Backwoods Brewing is a craft beer maker with deep roots in the Columbia Gorge. I worked with them to design their first can product line — a major leap for a small company. I updated their visual branding while maintaining their energetic, rustic style. We’ve continued to work together to create their bottle line, seasonal releases, sales materials, and more.
